Roger's Favourite Movies

My top 3 movies of all time

1. The Matrix

A groundbreaking sci-fi movie. It has set the standard for action sci-fi movies.

2. Up in the air

Just a poignant, beautifully acted movie about lonliness and human relationships.

3. Spiderman into the Spiderverse

Arguably the best comic book movie ever made, the artwork is amazing. A visual masterpiece.
